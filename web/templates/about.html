What is Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis?

Traditional sentiment analysis tells you whether a review is positive or negative overall. But Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis (ABSA) goes deeperâ€”it extracts what specific aspects customers are discussing and determines their sentiment towards each one.

For example, instead of just saying "This phone review is positive," ABSA can tell you: "Customers love the camera, but have concerns about battery life."
